ABOUT

The SWM Organization (T.SWM.O) stands as a distinguished conglomerate based in the United Republic of Tanzania (URT), with a commanding 51% share value in two groups of companies. Functioning as the sole entity overseeing its subsidiaries, T.SWM.O extends its operations into East African Cooperation (EAC) countries and is actively expanding into South Africa.

With a dedicated focus on excellence and ethical business practices, T.SWM.O has established itself as a leader in film production, logistics & e-commerce, real estate, and transport. The organization's commitment to corporate social responsibility plays a pivotal role in contributing to economic development, generating job opportunities, and enhancing the quality of life in Tanzania and East Africa.

Comprising two groups, MAzger Group and FarJoy Group, each housing three companies/institutions, T.SWM.O has earned a reputation for excellence, integrity, and innovation, solidifying its position as a premier conglomerate in Tanzania and the broader East African region. Looking forward, the organization envisions sustained growth, expansion, and diversification into sectors such as health and education, leveraging cutting-edge technology and equipment. Join us on this journey toward a future marked by continued success and positive impact.

Our Mission

Our mission at T.SWM.O is to empower communities through sustainable business practices, fostering economic growth, and creating a positive impact on society. Committed to delivering exceptional products and services, we aim to enhance the quality of life for people across East Africa and beyond, contributing to long-term prosperity and well-being in the region.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a pioneering force in the business world, setting new standards of excellence and innovation. We aspire to be recognized globally for our unwavering commitment to ethical business practices, social responsibility, and continuous advancement. T.SWM.O envisions a future where our impact reaches far and wide, contributing to the prosperity and well-being of communities we serve.

Our Core Values

T.SWM.O is founded on a bedrock of integrity, excellence, and collaboration. We prioritize customer satisfaction, embrace diversity, and commit to sustainable practices. These core values drive us to innovate, succeed as a team, and make a positive impact on the communities we serve.
